Abstract

The main objective of this application is to decrease the manual mistakes engaged in the commercial airline booking process during a PANDEMIC like in present times and create it practical for the clients to book the routes as when they require such that they can implement this application to create bookings, change bookings or terminate a particular booking. The main purpose for developing a Flight Booking System project is to provide all the information related to flights like booking, tickets, payment, flights etc with respect to restrictions and new guidlines of travelling imposed due to the pandemic situation of COVID-19 VIRUS.
